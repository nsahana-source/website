BUDT748 INDUSTRY PRACTICUM
Course Description:

The Capstone class is designed as an industry sponsored practicum project. It has two main objectives: First, the course provides students an opportunity to work on a real-world project where they will work with a sponsoring firm to use information technology to solve a real-world business problem. To accomplish this goal, the students will synthesize and integrate the skills and knowledge acquired in the MSIS program.
